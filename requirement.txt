1. Deepgram Nova-3 (Speech-to-Text)
Deepgram charges strictly per minute of live audio streamed over your API connection. 
Official Price: $0.0077 per minute  INR Conversion: ₹0.73 per minute
Cost for ONE 3-Minute Conversation: ₹2.19

2. Groq (Whisper Large v3 Turbo)(Speech to text)
Groq charges strictly per hour of transcribed audio, utilizing specialized LPU (Language Processing Unit) chips to deliver near-instantaneous processing speeds. 
Official Price: $0.04 per hour of audio  INR Conversion: ₹0.063 per minute (using 1 USD = ₹95)
Cost for ONE 3-Minute Conversation: ₹0.19 (19 paise)

Limitations of Groq Whisper:

a. No Live Audio Streaming:-At the time of evaluation, Groq Whisper is primarily positioned as a batch transcription service and provides fewer mature real-time streaming and telephony-focused capabilities compared to Deepgram.
This means you cannot stream audio chunk-by-chunk while a user is actively speaking.
The user has to finish their entire sentence, your app must wait for the audio file to stop recording, package it into a .mp3 or .wav file, upload the file to Groq, and wait for the transcription to return.

Deepgram Advantage: Supports continuous WebSocket-based streaming and can transcribe speech incrementally while the user is speaking, reducing conversational latency.
Groq Whisper is optimized primarily for fast transcription rather than telephony-focused real-time streaming. 
While it can achieve very low latency, providers such as Deepgram offer more mature streaming infrastructure, telephony integrations, speaker diarization, and production voice-agent tooling.

b. Potential rate limits depending on the selected plan and usage volume.
c. Limited Speaker Diarization Support (Identifying who spoke when)
d. Audio File Size Limitations – Larger audio files may require chunking or preprocessing before transcription.


3. GPT-4o mini (The Core LLM Brain)
OpenAI charges per "token" (roughly pieces of words).
A typical 3-minute back-and-forth conversation accumulates roughly 1,000 text tokens total between user questions and bot answers.  
Official Price: $0.15/1M input tokens & $0.60/1M output tokens  INR Conversion: A fraction of a paisa per single message.
Cost for ONE 3-Minute Conversation: ~₹0.01–₹0.05 depending on conversation length and token usage. (Extremely cheap)

4. Google Cloud TTS - Neural2 (Text-to-Speech)
Google charges based on the number of characters the bot speaks. In a 3-minute chat, the bot usually speaks around 1,500 characters of text.  Official Price: $16.00 per 1 Million characters (if you cross the free limit).  INR Conversion: ₹1.52 per 1,000 characters
Cost for ONE 3-Minute Conversation:~₹0.02–₹0.03
